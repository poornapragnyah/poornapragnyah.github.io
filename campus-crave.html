Campus Crave

Experience a revolution in campus dining with Campus Crave! Tired of long lines at college canteens? Say goodbye to wait times and hello to convenience. Simply pre-order your meal during class and collect it later without the hassle of queues. Campus Crave is here to transform your dining experience, making every mealtime a breeze! Built using the MERN stack this project is close to my heart as it solves a real world problem I face on a regular basis.
