What's the Weather

"What's the Weather" is a comprehensive weather application developed as our final project for the course "Problem solving with Python" in our first semester. This platform provides real-time weather updates and forecasts to users, catering to their meteorological needs. While I designed and crafted the engaging frontend using HTML and Vanilla CSS, my teammate harnessed the power of Django to build a robust backend system. Together, we've successfully brought to life a user-friendly weather information hub, allowing users to access accurate and up-to-date weather data with ease.
